What is AO?

AlmostOne is a Defi project, with the purpose of technological experimentation in the field of blockchain. AO is a basic ERC20 token with a 0.999999 supply.

What is AOF?

AOF is a Balancer SmartPool (AO, WBTC, ETH, USDC, DPI). AO gains trading volume from arbitrage opportunities between AO/ETH in uniswap and AOF, which increases fee returns for AOF holders and farmers.

Farming Liquidity

The AO Farming is an incentive to participate in AOF and a distribution mechanism: By adding liquidity to the fund, you receive AOF which can be locked for some time to receive free AO. 0.333 AO can be farmed.

Goals

1- Study how AO can peg its value to the overall crypto market just by being connected to different liquidity pools.

2- Study if rebalancing and arbitrage trading fees can make AOF more profitable than a simple buy-and-hold strategy.
